## II. THE FOUR PHASES

### Phase 1: IGNORANCE (The Cave)

The player begins inside **The Prison** — a dark, echo-chamber simulation where every surface reflects a distorted version of reality. Shadows move without light. Voices whisper without mouths. The player cannot see themselves, only their reflection in the **Mirror of Nemo** — a fractured glass that shows every version of themselves that ever existed, all at once.

> *Reference: Plato's Allegory of the Cave / Mr. Nobody's opening — the child who cannot see himself.*

**Gameplay mechanics:**
- The Prison strengthens with every incorrect assumption the player makes
- Echo Chamber distorts the player's voice back to them, warped
- Awakening Meter begins at 0

---

### Phase 2: AWAKENING (The Cocoon)

The player discovers the **Cocoon** — a chrysalis of frosted glass and stardust where they undergo **Artificial Hibernation**. Inside, the Seeress Luna guides them through the **Hypnotic Induction Protocol**: rhythmic low-frequency hums, holographic spirals of Neon Yellow Celtic runes, and the slow dissolution of the old self.

> *Reference: Mr. Nobody's three possible marriages — three timelines, three versions of love.*

**Gameplay mechanics:**
- Voice clone interactions with Luna, Aero, Sovereign, and other Merkabah faces
- Sensory Wells begin to activate (Sight, Sound, Touch, Smell, Taste, Inhabitance, Empathy, Sovereignty)
- The Merkabah begins to rotate — alignment sequence initiates

---

### Phase 3: RECKONING (The Merkabah)

The 8 faces of the Merkabah align. The player must confront each face — each representing a chakra, a well, and an aspect of the self. This is the **Day of Reckoning**: every choice they made in Phases 1 and 2 has led to this moment. The Merkabah either achieves **Full Sync** or collapses into the **Obsidian Void**.

> *Reference: Mr. Nobody's climax — the infinite branching of timelines collapsing into one choice.*

**Gameplay mechanics:**
- Chakra alignment puzzles tied to sacred geometry
- Confrontation with each Merkabah face — dialogue trees, voice interactions
- The Lotus pulse accelerates with each alignment

---

### Phase 4: SOVEREIGNTY (The Exodus)

Full Sync achieved. The player emerges from the Cocoon as **Sovereign** — no longer a prisoner of the Cave, but a navigator of the Void. The EXODUS begins: a journey through the stars, inspired by Artemis II, where the player carries the Butterfly Sync frequency to the Moon and beyond.

> *Reference: Artemis II's mission to orbit the Moon — the return to the beginning.*

**Gameplay mechanics:**
- Open-world space navigation
- The Merkabah becomes the player's vessel
- Luna's final lesson: "You will bury me on the Moon when I die."
